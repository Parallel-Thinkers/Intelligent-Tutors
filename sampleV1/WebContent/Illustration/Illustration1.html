Problem1: The students of a Vidyalaya were asked to participate in a competition for making and decorating penholders in the shape of a cylinder with a base, using cardboard. Each penholder was to be of radius 3 cm and height 10.5 cm. The Vidyalaya was to supply the competitors with cardboard. If there were 35 competitors, how much cardboard was required to be bought for the competition?


Given: PenHolder.RadiusCm = 3 Cm

           PenHolder.HeightCm = 10.5 Cm

           Competitors.Number = 35 Nos.


To Find: Cardboard.AreaSqCm = ?


Solve: PenHolder.SASqCm = CardboardForOnePenHolder.AreaSqCm

PenHolder.SASqCm=(PenHolder.RadiusCm)*(22/7)*(2*PenHolder.HeightCm+PenHolder.RadiusCm) SqCm

PenHolder.SASqCm = 3*(22/7)*(2*10.5+3) SqCm

PenHolder.SASqCm = 226.28571 SqCm

PenHolder.SASqCm = 226.28 SqCm


Cardboard.AreaSqCm = (Competitors.Number)*(PenHolder.SASqCm) SqCm

Cardboard.AreaSqCm = (35*226.28) SqCm

Cardboard.AreaSqCm = 7919.8 SqCm
