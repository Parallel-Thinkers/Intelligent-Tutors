Problem2: A gulab jamun, contains sugar syrup up to about 30% of its volume. Find approximately how much syrup would be found in 45 gulab jamuns, each shaped like a cylinder with two hemispherical ends with length 5 cm and diameter 2.8 cm.


Given: GulabjamunSugerSyrupVolume.Percentage = 30 %

           Gulabjamun.Number = 45 Nos.

           HemisphericalEnd.DiameterCm = 2.8 Cm    

           Gulabjamun.LengthCm = 5 Cm


To Find: GulabjamunSugerSyrupFor45Gulabjamun.VolumeCuCm = ?


HemisphericalEnd.RadiusCm = (HemisphericalEnd.DiameterCm/2) Cm

HemisphericalEnd.RadiusCm = 1.4 Cm


HemisphericalEnd.VolumeCuCm=(2/3)*(22/7)*(HemisphericalEnd.RadiusCm*HemisphericalEnd.RadiusCm*HemisphericalEnd.RadiusCm) CuCm


HemisphericalEnd.VolumeCuCm = (2/3)*(22/7)*(1.4*1.4*1.4) CuCm

HemisphericalEnd.VolumeCuCm = 5.749333 CuCm

HemisphericalEnd.VolumeCuCm = 5.75 CuCm


GulabjamunCylindericalPart.RadiusCm = HemisphericalEnd.RadiusCm

GulabjamunCylindericalPart.RadiusCm = 1.4 Cm


GulabjamunCylindericalPart.HeightCm = Gulabjamun.LengthCm

GulabjamunCylindericalPart.HeightCm = 5 Cm


GulabjamunCylindericalPart.VolumeCuCm=(22/7)*(GulabjamunCylindericalPart.RadiusCm*GulabjamunCylindericalPart.RadiusCm*GulabjamunCylindericalPart.HeightCm) CuCm

GulabjamunCylindericalPart.VolumeCuCm = (22/7)*(1.4*1.4*5) CuCm

GulabjamunCylindericalPart.VolumeCuCm = 30.8 CuCm


Gulabjamun.VolumeCuCm=(GulabjamunCylindericalPart.VolumeCuCm+2*HemisphericalEnd.VolumeCuCm) CuCm


Gulabjamun.VolumeCuCm = (30.8+2*5.75) CuCm

Gulabjamun.VolumeCuCm = 42.3 CuCm


GulabjamunSugerSyrup.VolumeCuCm=(30/100)*(Gulabjamun.VolumeCuCm) CuCm


GulabjamunSugerSyrup.VolumeCuCm = (3/10)*(42.3) CuCm

GulabjamunSugerSyrup.VolumeCuCm = 12.69 CuCm


GulabjamunSugerSyrupFor45Gulabjamun.VolumeCuCm=(45*GulabjamunSugerSyrup.VolumeCuCm) CuCm


GulabjamunSugerSyrupFor45Gulabjamun.VolumeCuCm = (45*12.69) CuCm


GulabjamunSugerSyrupFor45Gulabjamun.VolumeCuCm= 571.05 CuCm
