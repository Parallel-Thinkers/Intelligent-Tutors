Solving Mode Help

  1. Solving mode has various parts such as Schematic Knowledge, Semantic Knowledge, Formula, Image, Given, To Prove and Solve.
  2. The Schematic Knowledge tab will display the problem schema of that particular type and this will help you to learn how to identify a problem type for a given problem when you are not working with the system. This schema also provides idea of the general points which you should look for to solve that particular problem.
  3. The Semantic Knowledge tab shows the connection of schematic knowledge with the domain semantics of that particular problem. It will also help you to understand the connection between linguistic terms in real world scenario and mathematical terms in the domain.
  4. Image tab will show you the image for that particular problem if it has an image.
  5. Formula tab will display list of all the formulas which you might want to use in that particular problem. This will help to reduce mental focus on memorization and increase the focus on thinking.
  6. Every problem has some given items and some things to find. You need to first write the Given items and To Find items in the space provided so that you can proceed for solving the problem. These items and the text in the solving mode needs to be written in a particular format. Please see the illustration in the illustration tab and find text entering help in the Text entering help bullet.
  7. After completion of any problem to go to next problem you have to click on Home tab.
  8. For every wrong entry in Given, To Find and Solve, you will get a feedback. The feedback depends on whether you have not entered unit or entered wrong unit or the text entered is grammatically (the way we have defined in the text entering help) wrong or wrong answer.
  9. Each correct text entry will get printed in the respective area of Given, To Prove and Solve.
  10. Correct entry of the final answer of the problem will provide you a feedback pop-up of “Well Done!”. After clicking on the “Ok” of the feedback pop-up your final answer will be printed and then you will be able to move to the next problem by clicking on Home.
